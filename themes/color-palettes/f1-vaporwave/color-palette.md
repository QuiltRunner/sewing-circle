# F1 Vaporwave Color Palette

This palette captures the neon, pastel motorsport vibe of the **F1 Vaporwave** theme.

## F1 Vaporwave Palette

- `#F79AD9 - Light Pink (Soft neon pink)`
- `#C78BFF - Lavender (Light, dreamy purple with cool tones)`
- `#B23CFF - Magenta Purple (Punchy, saturated purple)`
- `#FFB4D9 - Rose Pink (Pastel bubblegum)`
- `#66E3FF - Light Cyan (Bright and cool cyan) `
- `#8B5CDB - Purple (Rich mid-purple with a slightly muted tone)`
- `#A85D87 - Mauve (Muted pink-mauve with a warm tint)`
- `#3B154C - Deep Plum (Dark purple with a saturated cool undertone)`

## Notes
- These colors are approximate values pulled from the artwork
- Use them to build matching themes (Kvantum, Starship, Fastfetch, KDE accents, icons, etc.)
